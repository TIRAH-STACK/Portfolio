Career Timeline

2026 (Immediate): Foundation & Validation

  • Complete degree in B.CS (Network Security).
  • Focus on hands-on lab practice using Wireshark and Linux to bridge the gap between networking theory and security operations.

2026 — 2027: Entry-Level SOC Analyst

  • Secure an entry-level position as a SOC Analyst (L1).
  • Focus on triaging security alerts, monitoring SIEM dashboards (e.g., Splunk/ELK), and performing initial packet analysis using Wireshark.
  • Deepen Linux CLI proficiency to conduct endpoint investigations.

2027 — 2029: Advanced SOC Operations

  • Advance to a SOC Analyst (L2) or Incident Responder role.
  • Pursue CompTIA CySA+ or BTL1 certification to master threat hunting and incident response workflows.
  • Develop skills in security automation by using Python to automate repetitive alert filtering tasks.

2029 — 2031: Senior SOC Analyst / Security Lead

  • Transition into a Senior SOC Analyst or Threat Hunter role.
  • Lead complex incident investigations, mentor junior analysts, and contribute to the optimization of SOC playbooks.
